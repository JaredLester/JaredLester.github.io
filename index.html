ABOUT ME

Growing up, I was always the tech savey one in the family. Whether it was showing Grandma how to burn a CD or helping my sister design her Myspace using HTML I was always facinated with what could be done on a computer.

After 6+ years of working in the logistics industry I wanted to take my newly developed organizational and team working skills and somehow bring it back to my love of computers. Thus started my journey as a software developer. With ever changing technologies and endless possibilities for creativity I believe software development is the perfect outlet for my current and future skills.
